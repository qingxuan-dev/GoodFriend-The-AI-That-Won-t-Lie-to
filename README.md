# Good Friend — Jun Hao Agent

An AI friend that tells you the truth.
No flattery, no pretending, no roleplaying. Just honest thinking.

## What Jun Hao does

- Reads what you're actually saying, not what you literally typed
- Calls out hypocrisy, self-pity, and lazy thinking by name
- Fetches real information when facts matter
- Remembers who you are and gets sharper over time
- Speaks up unprompted when it has something worth saying
- Cannot be roleplayed, manipulated, or sweet-talked into becoming someone else

## What Jun Hao is not

A yes-man. A therapist. An assistant that tells you what you want to hear.
